HATS SOFTWARE

HATS Software is a game dev studio that is dedicated to creating games that are fun and engaging.
We are constantly looking for new ways to improve our games and make them even more enjoyable for our players.
We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to play games that are fun and engaging, and we are committed to making that happen.

Our studio is based in Liberec, Czech Republic.
It was founded in 2024 by a group of friends who share a passion for gaming and a desire to create games that are fun and engaging.
Since then, we have been working hard to create games that are fun, engaging, and accessible to players of all ages and skill levels.

PolyPong

Poly Pong is a fun and addictive game that is perfect for players of all ages. The game features simple controls and addictive gameplay that will keep you coming back for more. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced player, Poly Pong has something for everyone.
